Canada News Sites Paywall Bypass
================================

Canada News Sites Paywall Bypass is a Google Chrome extension to allow unlimited article reading on the following news sites for free:

* Edmonton Sun
* Calgary Sun
* Ottawa Sun
* Toronto Sun
* Winnipeg Sun
* Calgary Herald
* Edmonton Journal
* Montreal Gazette
* Leader Post
* The StarPhoenix
* The Province
* The Vancouver Sun
* Times Colonist
* The Windsor Star
* National Post
* Financial Post
* The Western Star
